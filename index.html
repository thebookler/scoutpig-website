ScoutPig: The Ultimate Barcode Scanning & Profit Analysis App

ScoutPig is a powerful mobile app designed for Amazon sellers, resellers, and entrepreneurs who want instant profitability insights when sourcing products. Simply scan any barcode or enter a product’s identifier, and ScoutPig retrieves real-time Amazon marketplace data, including current pricing, sales rank, and competitive offers. With built-in profit calculations, the app factors in shipping costs, fees, and selling method (MF or FBA) to determine your exact profit margins. Whether you’re in a retail store, at a thrift shop, or sourcing online, ScoutPig gives you the data you need to make fast, smart buying decisions—helping you maximize profits and stay ahead of the competition.

Available soon on the iOS App Store.
