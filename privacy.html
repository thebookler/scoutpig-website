Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ScoutPig! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and website.

By using ScoutPig, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at support@scoutpig.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address (for login and marketing communications)
  • Name (for account identification)
  • Location (for service personalization)
  • Payment Information (if applicable)

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Search Lookups & Product Scans: We store product searches and ISBN/GPC lookups to enhance our product database.
  • Usage Data: We collect analytics about how you use our app.
  • Device & Log Data: Includes IP address, device type, and browser version.

2.3 Third-Party Data Collection

  • Google Analytics & Firebase Analytics: To analyze user behavior.
  • Firebase Authentication & Cloud Storage: For authentication and secure data storage.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the ScoutPig app.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Send marketing emails (opt-out available).
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We share necessary data with:

  • Google & Firebase Services (for authentication and analytics).
  • Payment Processors (if applicable).

We do not share personal data with advertisers.

5. How Users Can Control Their Data

Account Deletion: Uninstalling the app will delete your account and associated data.

Email Preferences: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

Contact for Data Requests: support@scoutpig.com.

6. Data Security

We use Firebase Cloud with encrypted storage and secure authentication to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

ScoutPig is open to all ages, but we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notifications.
